LOCAL RESTAURANTS + ARTISTS, TRANSFORMING ‘STREEATERIES’ INTO STREET ART

The NEIGHBORHOOD CURBSIDE CANVAS PROJECT
is a volunteer neighborhood-recovery effort, uniting local artists with struggling restaurants to transform pop-up dining patios into street art, for the purposes of driving greater interest, energy and business back into our COVID-coping communities.

ABC Eyewitness News 7
October 6, 2020
